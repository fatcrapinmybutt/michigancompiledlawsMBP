Anderson v Oakland County Clerk, 419 Mich 313, 353 NW2d 448 (1984).This act violates the second sentence of Const 1963, art 4, § 24, and is therefore unconstitutional. Anderson v Oakland County Clerk, 419 Mich 142, 350 NW2d 232 (1984).
